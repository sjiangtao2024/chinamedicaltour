Citizens of the following countries holding ordinary passports can enter China without a visa for business, tourism, family visits, or transit for up to 15 days (some extended to 30 days).

No Application Needed

Just book your flight and fly. Show your passport at border control.

Transit

144-Hour Visa-Free Transit

Ideal for Medical Tourism! If you transit through China to a third country/region (e.g., USA -> Beijing -> Hong Kong -> USA), you can stay for 6 days (144 hours) without a visa.

How it works

  1. Book a ticket: Country A (Origin) → China (Transit) → Country C (Destination).
  2. Country A and C must be different (Hong Kong/Macau count as different regions).
  3. Upon arrival, apply for "144-hour Temporary Entry Permit" at the border.
  4. Enjoy your 6-day medical checkup and cultural tour!
