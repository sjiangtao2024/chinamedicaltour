Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

To facilitate your medical tour, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Identity Data: Name, passport number, date of birth, nationality.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, WeChat/WhatsApp ID.
  • Health Data: Medical history, check-up reports (only as strictly necessary for hospital appointments).
  • Travel Data: Flight details, visa information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Booking appointments with our partner hospitals.
  • Arranging airport transfers and accommodation.
  • Providing visa invitation letters.
  • Processing payments.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your privacy. We do not sell your data.

We only share necessary data with:

  • Medical Providers: To confirm your appointment and medical needs.
  • Government Authorities: If required for visa processing or by law.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, access controls) to protect your sensitive health and personal information. Data is stored on secure servers and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Please contact us at info@chinamedicaltour.org for any privacy-related inquiries.
