Why You Must Prepare This Before You Fly

China is the world's first truly cashless society. Electronic payment isn't just an option; it's the heartbeat of daily life.

From 5-star hotels to tiny street food stalls, from high-speed trains to renting a shared bicycle, QR codes are everywhere. Carrying cash can actually be inconvenient as many vendors struggle to find change.

Setting up Alipay or WeChat Pay is not just for paying your medical bills—it is your digital passport to convenience. It empowers you to:

  • Buy coffee or street snacks
  • Pay for DiDi (China's Uber) & Taxis
  • Ride Subways & Buses
  • Shop at Convenience Stores

How to Set Up Alipay

1

Download the Alipay App

Available on Apple App Store and Google Play Store. We recommend downloading before arriving in China.

→ App Store → Google Play
2

Register with Your Phone Number

Use your international phone number. Enter the SMS verification code, then create a login password and payment password.

3

Complete Identity Verification

Navigate to "Me" → "Identity Verification". Upload your passport and complete facial recognition. This unlocks higher limits ($5,000 per transaction).

Pro Tip: Name Format

Enter your name EXACTLY as it appears on the bottom lines (machine-readable zone) of your passport. Usually: SURNAME GIVENNAME.
Important: Verification is required for transactions over $500
4

Add Your International Card

Link your Visa, Mastercard, Diners Club, Discover, or JCB card. No Chinese bank account needed!

✓

Ready to Pay!

You can now pay for your medical tour package and use Alipay anywhere in China.

How to Set Up WeChat Pay

1

Download WeChat

Download the WeChat app from your app store. Set up before your trip to China for convenience.

→ App Store → Google Play
2

Create WeChat Account

Register using your international phone number and verify with SMS code.

3

Navigate to WeChat Pay

Go to "Me" → "Services" → "Wallet" → "Bank Cards" → "Add New Bank Card"

4

Add International Card & Verify

Enter your card details (Visa, MC, AmEx, JCB, etc.), billing address, and personal information. Upload your passport for identity verification.

Pro Tip: Name Format

Use the SURNAME GIVENNAME format (all caps) to match your passport's electronic data for instant approval.
Fast verification: Passport verification typically completes in "seconds".
5

Set Payment PIN

Create a 6-digit payment PIN for secure transactions.

✓

All Set!

Start paying for your medical packages and enjoy cashless convenience throughout China.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Chinese bank account?

No! You can use your international credit or debit card (Visa, Mastercard, AmEx, JCB, etc.) directly with both Alipay and WeChat Pay.

What is the maximum I can pay at once?

After completing identity verification with your passport, you can pay up to $5,000 per transaction and $50,000 annually with both platforms.

Are there any transaction fees?

Alipay: Transaction fees are determined by your card issuing bank.
WeChat Pay: Transactions ≤ ¥200 (~$28) are free. Transactions above ¥200 incur a 3% fee (refunded proportionally if you get a refund).

How long does registration take?

Registration typically takes 5-10 minutes. Identity verification with your passport is fast—often completed instantly or within minutes.

How do I pay for my medical package?

After selecting your package, we'll send you a payment QR code via email or WhatsApp. Simply scan it with your Alipay or WeChat Pay app to complete payment securely.

Is it safe and secure?

Absolutely! Both Alipay and WeChat Pay use bank-level encryption and are trusted by over 1 billion users worldwide. All transactions are protected and monitored for fraud.

Can I get a refund if needed?

Yes. Refunds will be returned to your payment account. With WeChat Pay, transaction fees are also refunded proportionally. Please review our refund policy for full details.
