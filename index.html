Easy Entry: Understanding the Latest Visa Policies

15/30-Day Visa-Free Entry

Ideal for short-term procedures like health check-ups, dental work, or consultations. Available to citizens of France, Germany, Spain, Australia, New Zealand, Thailand, Singapore, and more.

10-Day Transit Visa-Free

A strategic option for citizens from 54 countries including the US, UK, and Canada. Perfect for combining minor surgery with a multi-destination Asia trip, as it requires travel to a third country/region.

Long-term Visa

Required for complex, long-term treatments like oncology or major surgery. We provide the official hospital invitation letter to facilitate your application.
